Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of geometry, analysis, and data science, with a particular focus on geometric and probabilistic structures arising in optimal transport and dynamical systems.

I am interested in mathematical formulations that bridge theoretical and computational perspectives — such as Gromov–Wasserstein geometry, information geometry, and their applications to biological data.
